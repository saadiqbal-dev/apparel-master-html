Food Industry Workwear

  1. Home
  2. Food Industry
  3. Food Industry Workwear

Apparelmaster offers a wide range of food industry workwear, which when integrated with a garment-cleaning programme, helps businesses meet and exceed hygiene requirements.

Businesses in the food and hospitality industries must meet increasingly strict standards for cleanliness and hygiene. As specialists in supplying and maintaining workwear for food and seafood processing, Pryor's Apparelmaster recognises the importance of complying with food safety policies and using MAF-approved cleaning products.

Our workwear range is designed for food handling and preparation, including dust coats, trousers, jerkins, and overalls. We also offer specialist clothing for hospitality roles, such as chefs' uniforms and aprons.
